iron man 3
The third part of the highly successful Marvel series; The film follows Tony Stark or Iron Man (Robert Downey Jr.) as he faces off against a super enemy with unlimited potential, Iron Man finds his world completely destroyed by this enemy, but he rises again and embarks on a terrifying quest to find everyone he was connected to. During this journey, you will test the hardness of Iron Man and his true metal. Lonely in the face of this menace, Stark relies on his instinct, genius, and machines to protect everyone he loves. During the battle, Stark begins to unravel the answer to the question that has haunted him for years: Did the Iron Suit make him, or did he make it?
